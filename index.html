About Our School

Excellence is Our Pride Since 2007

Our History & Mission

St. Peter's Aterait Secondary School was established in 2007 with a vision to provide quality education to the community of Busia County. Located in Teso South sub-county, Apegei location, Aterait sub-location, we are committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals.

Our mission is to provide holistic education that develops students academically, spiritually, and socially, preparing them for future challenges and opportunities.
